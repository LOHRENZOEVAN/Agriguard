    About Us

    Agriguard is a climate technology startup equipping farmers, agritech firms, and aggregators with weather-based advisory, personalized production risk scores, and tailored micro-insurance and agri-service recommendations for confident harvests.

    Climate change and its effect on food production.

    Rural farmers often face financial risks due to unpredictable weather conditions, making it challenging for them to secure their production cycles. Accessing insurance services that cater to disaster risk reduction are almost totally absent.

    Services

    Advisory service

    Farmers are given advice based on past information and predicted weather data on what would give them great output

    Production Risk Scoring

    Agriguard doesn't offer one-size-fits-all solutions. We believe in the power of personalization. Our platform generates farmer risk scores that are tailored to individual profiles, accounting for specific geographical and operational nuances. This personalized approach enables farmers to make informed decisions based on a risk assessment that aligns precisely with their unique circumstances, fostering a deeper level of trust in our services.

    Climate forecasting

    We provide personalized weather alerts to the farmers that will prompt the farmers to get insurance to protect their livelihood.
